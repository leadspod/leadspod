Email Relay Server?

Our relay server is at the heart of our infrastructure and allows you to precisely schedule and manage multiple email accounts. It also schedules and creates the emails for our email warmup service.

When you order an Email Relay Server, it will take a few hours to set up. You will get an email with all the detail on how to connect it to the Mautic software. Then, you will be able to use all the email addresses you added to the warmup service.
